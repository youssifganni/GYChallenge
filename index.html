The 6:00 AM 365 Days GY Challenge

Wake up. Work out. Make it happen.

Work with a team every morning that is dedicated to help you wake up at 6:00 AM to get your day started early.

The goal is to help form new habits. Meet up every day at 6:45 AM and by starting your day with a intense workout.

This is a free MeetUp experience offered by Gilles & Youssif designed to help change and shape your future.

Get started tomorrow morning with your GY Challenge at one of our personal favourite Partnering Gyms.

"I rarely find time nowadays to socialize and doing something good for myself. I am always so preoccupied by my work that I become lost in it. Starting the GY challenge has to be a once in a lifetime opportunity to turn my life around, inspire others, as well as get inspired by others. This is how it works. This challenge not only enables me to better myself, but also to keep meeting new people on a weekly basis and setting new goals."

-Youssif Ganni - Full Stack Web Engineer Student/frequent weightlifting since 2008
